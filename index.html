Projects

DvG

DvG (Dwarves versus Goblins) is a strategy role playing game about the eternal struggle between order and chaos and the ways in which those forces shape a world and its inhabitants.

Leveraging procudural world and history generation and a unique turn based battle system, players will explore the world of DvG; interacting with it's inhabitants, each with motives of their own.

DvG is currently in developement and not yet released to the public. Keep an eye on our itch page for more info.
