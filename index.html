DvG

DVG (DWARVES VERSUS GOBLINS) IS A STRATEGY ROLE PLAYING GAME ABOUT THE ETERNAL STRUGGLE BETWEEN ORDER AND CHAOS AND THE WAYS IN WHICH THOSE FORCES SHAPE A WORLD AND ITS INHABITANTS.

LEVERAGING PROCEDURAL WORLD AND HISTORY GENERATION AND A UNIQUE TURN BASED BATTLE SYSTEM, PLAYERS WILL EXPLORE THE WORLD OF DVG; INTERACTING WITH IT'S INHABITANTS, EACH WITH MOTIVES OF THEIR OWN.

DVG IS CURRENTLY IN DEVELOPEMENT AND NOT YET RELEASED TO THE PUBLIC. FOLLOW US ON ITCH.IO FOR UPDATES.
