PROJECTS

DvG

DvG (Dwarves versus Goblins) is a strategy role playing game about the eternal struggle between order and chaos and the ways in which those forces shape a world and it's inhabitants.

Leveraging procedural world and history generation and a unique turn based battle system, players will explore the world of DvG; interacting with it's inhabitants, each with motives and perspectives of their own.

DvG is currently in active developement and not yet released to the public. Want to play or support anyway? Check out our patreon! Follow us on itch.io for updates on the full release.
